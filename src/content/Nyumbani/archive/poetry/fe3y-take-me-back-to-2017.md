In-deed I tried my best, taking chances to find the vices 

But baffled I was, baffled I’m still 

They claim love is blind….

I digress.

I think you’re perfect 

…. NAY…. 

I’m sure you’re perfect 

Perfect in any imaginable way 

I find your visage pretty cute you know, 

And your personality, totally stellar 

Splendid would be an understatement. 

Honestly, I like you…pretty much I’d say 

And imagining of a novel, 

I know we’d meet on the very first page 

Coz I’ve got a feeling ours would be great 

And just to predict the last lines I’d say. 

*He turned at her cutely* 

*Sentimental eyes, pulled closer* 

*And brushed his lips against hers.* 

*Obsessively they belonged,* 

*Belonged to their ever after* 

*Which had dawned* 

*Bright &* 

*warm*
