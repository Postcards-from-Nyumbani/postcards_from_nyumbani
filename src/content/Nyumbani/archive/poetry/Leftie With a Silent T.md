I once met a chic<br>
who was socially awkward,<br>
and introverted as me<br>

With her fair share of melanin<br>
she was elite<br>

I’d hugged her a couple of times<br>
thrice if I’m right<br>
it felt nice<br>
pocket size just the way I liked<br>

I happened to get out of sight<br>
and in my solitude<br>
she was the first in mind<br>

the only one alive<br>

I still think of her at times<br>
more often at night<br>
when darkness shines bright<br>
and memories mere psychedelic plights.<br>
